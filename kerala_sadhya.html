Kerala Sadhya

Ingredients

  • Rice - 2 cups
  • Sambar - 1 serving
  • Avial - 1 serving
  • Thoran (Cabbage/Beans) - 1 serving
  • Pickles (Mango/Pineapple) - 1 tbsp
  • Rasam - 1 serving
  • Parippu Curry (Dal) - 1 serving
  • Fried Pappadam - 2 pieces
  • Curd - 1/2 cup
  • Banana (Pazham) - 1

Preparation Steps

  1. Prepare all the dishes listed in the ingredients above.
  2. Serve each dish in small portions along with rice on a banana leaf.
  3. Arrange sambar, avial, thoran, parippu curry, rasam, and curd in individual sections.
  4. Add pickles and pappadam on the side.
  5. Place banana on the side as a sweet addition.
  6. Enjoy the traditional Kerala feast.
