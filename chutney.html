Coconut Chutney

A creamy, refreshing, and mildly spiced chutney made from fresh coconut — perfect as a side for dosa, idli, or vada. A staple in every South Indian kitchen.

Prep Time: 10 mins
Cook Time: 5 mins
Servings: 4
Breakfast Vegetarian South Indian Side Dish

Instructions

STEP 1

Grind grated coconut, green chili, roasted gram dal, and salt with some water into a smooth paste.

STEP 2

Adjust consistency with water as needed and transfer to a bowl.

STEP 3

For tempering: Heat oil, splutter mustard seeds, then add dried red chilies and curry leaves. Pour over the chutney and mix.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup grated coconut (fresh)
  • 2 green chilies (adjust to taste)
  • 2 tbsp roasted gram dal (pottu kadala)
  • Salt to taste
  • Water as needed
  • 1 tsp coconut oil (for tempering)
  • 1/2 tsp mustard seeds
  • 2 dried red chilies
  • 1 sprig curry leaves

Tools

  • Blender or small mixie
  • Small frying pan
  • Spatula
  • Serving bowl
