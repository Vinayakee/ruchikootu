Ghee Rice

A fragrant and rich rice dish made with basmati rice, pure ghee, and whole spices. Perfect with curries or fried accompaniments, this Kerala-style neychoru is a festival favorite!

Prep Time: 10 mins
Cook Time: 25 mins
Servings: 4
Lunch Vegetarian Kerala Fragrant

Instructions

STEP 1

Wash and soak basmati rice for 20 minutes. Drain and keep aside.

STEP 2

In a large pot, heat ghee. Add bay leaf, cinnamon, cardamom, cloves, and star anise. Sauté for a minute until fragrant.

STEP 3

Add thinly sliced onions and sauté until golden brown. Reserve some fried onions for garnish.

STEP 4

Add drained rice and sauté gently for 2-3 minutes. Add hot water and salt.

STEP 5

Cover and cook on low flame until rice is fluffy and water is absorbed. Garnish with fried onions, cashews, and raisins sautéed in ghee.

Ingredients

  • 1.5 cups basmati rice
  • 2 tbsp ghee
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 2 cardamom pods
  • 3 cloves
  • 1-inch cinnamon stick
  • 1 small star anise
  • 1 onion, thinly sliced
  • 2.5 cups hot water
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tbsp fried cashews
  • 1 tbsp raisins

Tools

  • Heavy-bottomed pot or rice cooker
  • Spatula
  • Measuring cups
  • Strainer
  • Small pan (for frying garnishes)
