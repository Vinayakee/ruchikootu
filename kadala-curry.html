Kadala Curry

A traditional Kerala delicacy, this black chickpea curry is rich, earthy, and perfectly spiced — usually paired with puttu or appam for a soulful start to the day.

Prep Time: 15 mins
Cook Time: 35 mins
Servings: 4
Breakfast Vegetarian Kerala Spicy

Instructions

STEP 1

Soak black chickpeas overnight. Pressure cook with salt and water for 5–6 whistles until soft.

STEP 2

In a pan, dry roast coconut until golden brown. Add coriander, fennel, chili powder, and garam masala. Grind into a smooth paste once cooled.

STEP 3

In another pan, sauté mustard seeds, curry leaves, onions, green chilies, and ginger-garlic paste until golden.

STEP 4

Add the coconut paste and cooked chickpeas with water as needed. Simmer until thick and flavorful.

STEP 5

Garnish with fresh curry leaves. Serve hot with puttu, appam, or idiyappam.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup black chickpeas (kadala), soaked overnight
  • 1/2 cup grated coconut
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 1 tsp mustard seeds
  • 2 green chilies, slit
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tsp chili powder
  • 1 tsp coriander powder
  • 1/2 tsp garam masala
  • 1/2 tsp fennel seeds
  • Curry leaves
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tbsp coconut oil

Tools

  • Pressure cooker
  • Grinder or blender
  • Deep-bottomed pan
  • Wooden spatula
  • Measuring cups/spoons
