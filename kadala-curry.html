Kadala Curry

Ingredients

  • Black chickpeas (kadala) - 1 cup
  • Onion - 1, chopped
  • Tomato - 1, chopped
  • Ginger-garlic paste - 1 tsp
  • Coconut - 1/2 cup, grated
  • Chili powder - 1 tsp
  • Coriander powder - 2 tsp
  • Turmeric - 1/4 tsp
  • Mustard seeds - 1/2 tsp
  • Curry leaves - a few
  • Oil - 2 tbsp
  • Salt - to taste

Preparation Steps

  1. Pressure cook chickpeas till soft.
  2. Roast coconut till brown, grind to a paste.
  3. Heat oil, crackle mustard, saut√© onions, ginger-garlic, tomato.
  4. Add spices and coconut paste, cook well.
  5. Add chickpeas, simmer and serve!
