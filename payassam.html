Payassam

Ingredients

  • Rice - 1/4 cup
  • Water - 1 1/2 cups
  • Milk - 2 cups
  • Sugar - 1/2 cup
  • Cardamom - 4 pods, crushed
  • Cashew nuts - 2 tbsp
  • Raisins - 2 tbsp
  • Ghee - 2 tbsp

Preparation Steps

  1. In a pan, dry roast the rice for a few minutes until it becomes slightly golden.
  2. In another pan, bring water to a boil, add the roasted rice, and cook until the rice is soft.
  3. Add milk and sugar to the cooked rice. Let it cook on medium heat, stirring occasionally, until the mixture thickens.
  4. Fry cashew nuts and raisins in ghee and add them to the payassam. Add crushed cardamom for fragrance.
  5. Once the payassam reaches a creamy consistency, remove it from the heat and serve hot or chilled.
