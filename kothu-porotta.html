Kothu Parotta

Ingredients

  • Parotta - 2, shredded
  • Onion - 1, chopped
  • Tomato - 1, chopped
  • Ginger-garlic paste - 1 tbsp
  • Green chilies - 2, chopped
  • Egg - 1, beaten
  • Chili powder - 1 tsp
  • Curry leaves - few
  • Oil - 2 tbsp
  • Salt - to taste

Preparation Steps

  1. Heat oil, saut√© onions, ginger-garlic, and tomatoes.
  2. Add scrambled egg and shredded parotta. Mix well.
  3. Season with chili powder, curry leaves, and salt.
