Kappa Meen Curry

Ingredients

  • Fish (any firm fish) - 500g
  • Boiled Tapioca (Kappa) - 2 cups
  • Onion - 1 large, sliced
  • Tomatoes - 2, chopped
  • Ginger-garlic paste - 1 tbsp
  • Curry leaves - a handful
  • Green chilies - 3, slit
  • Chili powder - 1 tbsp
  • Coriander powder - 1 tbsp
  • Turmeric powder - 1/2 tsp
  • Coconut milk - 1 cup
  • Salt - to taste
  • Mustard seeds - 1 tsp
  • Coconut oil - 2 tbsp

Preparation Steps

  1. In a pan, heat coconut oil and splutter mustard seeds. Add curry leaves, onions, and green chilies. Sauté until golden.
  2. Add ginger-garlic paste and sauté until the raw smell disappears.
  3. Now, add chopped tomatoes, chili powder, coriander powder, and turmeric powder. Cook for 5 minutes until tomatoes soften.
  4. Add the fish pieces and salt. Pour in some water and cook until the fish is tender.
  5. Once the fish is cooked, add coconut milk and let it simmer for another 10 minutes.
  6. Serve the fish curry hot with boiled tapioca (Kappa).
