Puttu

Ingredients

  • Rice flour - 2 cups
  • Grated coconut - 1 cup
  • Salt - to taste
  • Water - as needed

Preparation Steps

  1. Mix rice flour with salt and sprinkle water to moisten.
  2. Layer rice flour and coconut alternately in puttu maker.
  3. Steam for 5-7 minutes till cooked.
  4. Serve hot with kadala curry or banana.
