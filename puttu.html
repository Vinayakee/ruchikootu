Puttu

A beloved Kerala breakfast classic made with steamed rice flour and grated coconut, usually paired with kadala curry or ripe bananas. Soft, fluffy, and simple!

Prep Time: 10 mins
Cook Time: 10 mins
Servings: 2–3
Breakfast Vegetarian Kerala Steamed

Instructions

STEP 1

Take roasted rice flour in a bowl. Add salt to taste. Sprinkle water little by little and mix using fingers to form a moist, crumbly texture.

STEP 2

When the flour holds shape when pressed but crumbles easily, it’s ready. Rest it for 10 minutes covered with a cloth.

STEP 3

In a puttu maker, layer grated coconut first, then rice flour, and alternate until filled. Finish with coconut on top.

STEP 4

Steam the puttu on a pressure cooker or steamer for 7–10 minutes or until you see steam coming out and the puttu is firm.

STEP 5

Serve hot with kadala curry, banana, or sugar and ghee.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup roasted rice flour (puttu podi)
  • 1/2 cup grated coconut
  • Salt to taste
  • Water (as needed)

Tools

  • Puttu maker (cylindrical or cheratta style)
  • Mixing bowl
  • Steamer or pressure cooker
  • Measuring cup
