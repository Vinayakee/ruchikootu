Unniyappam

Unniyappam is a beloved Kerala snack made from rice flour, banana, jaggery, and coconut. Fried in ghee or oil, it's a festive treat with a soft, chewy center and crispy edge.

Prep Time: 20 mins
Cook Time: 25 mins
Servings: 15–20 pieces
Sweet Snack Traditional Kerala

Instructions

STEP 1

Melt jaggery in 1/2 cup water, strain and let cool. Mash banana in a bowl until smooth.

STEP 2

Mix rice flour, mashed banana, jaggery syrup, grated coconut, cardamom powder, and a pinch of baking soda into a thick batter. Rest for 30 minutes.

STEP 3

Heat ghee or oil in an appam pan. Pour spoonfuls of batter into each mold. Cook on low flame till golden on both sides.

STEP 4

Remove and drain on paper towel. Serve warm or at room temperature.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup rice flour
  • 1 large ripe banana
  • 1/2 cup grated jaggery
  • 1/4 cup grated coconut
  • 1/2 tsp cardamom powder
  • Pinch of baking soda
  • Ghee or oil for frying
  • Water as needed

Tools

  • Appam chatti (unniyappam pan)
  • Mixing bowl
  • Spatula
  • Measuring cups
  • Strainer
