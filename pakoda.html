Pakoda

A crispy tea-time snack made with onions and spices coated in gram flour and deep-fried to perfection. A favorite during rainy days in Kerala homes.

Prep Time: 10 mins
Cook Time: 15 mins
Servings: 4
Snack Tea-time Crispy Kerala

Instructions

STEP 1

In a bowl, mix thinly sliced onions, green chilies, curry leaves, ginger, and salt. Crush lightly with hands to release moisture.

STEP 2

Add besan (gram flour), rice flour, chili powder, turmeric, and mix gently without adding water. The onion moisture helps bind it.

STEP 3

If mixture is too dry, sprinkle a few drops of water. Heat oil in a pan.

STEP 4

Drop small clusters into hot oil. Fry on medium heat till golden and crispy. Do not overcrowd.

STEP 5

Drain on paper towel. Serve hot with tea or chutney.

Ingredients

  • 2 onions, thinly sliced
  • 2 green chilies, chopped
  • 1 tsp ginger, grated
  • 1 sprig curry leaves
  • 3/4 cup besan (gram flour)
  • 2 tbsp rice flour
  • 1/2 tsp chili powder
  • 1/4 tsp turmeric
  • Salt to taste
  • Oil for deep frying

Tools

  • Mixing bowl
  • Deep frying pan
  • Slotted spoon
  • Paper towels
