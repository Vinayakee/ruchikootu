Squid Fry

Ingredients

  • Squid - 500g, cleaned
  • Onion - 1, chopped
  • Ginger-garlic paste - 1 tbsp
  • Chili powder - 1 tsp
  • Curry leaves - few
  • Oil - 2 tbsp
  • Salt - to taste

Preparation Steps

  1. Heat oil, saut√© onions and ginger-garlic paste.
  2. Add squid and cook with spices until tender.
  3. Finish with curry leaves. Serve hot!
