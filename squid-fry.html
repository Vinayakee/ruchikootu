Squid Fry

A spicy and crispy Kerala-style calamari fry, loaded with flavors of pepper, garlic, and curry leaves. Perfect as a side dish or appetizer for seafood lovers.

Prep Time: 15 mins
Cook Time: 20 mins
Servings: 3–4
Seafood Kerala Spicy Starter

Instructions

STEP 1

Clean and slice squid into rings. Rinse well and drain excess water.

STEP 2

Marinate squid with chili powder, turmeric, crushed garlic, pepper, salt, and lemon juice. Let it rest for 20 minutes.

STEP 3

Heat coconut oil in a pan. Add curry leaves and sliced onions (optional) and sauté briefly.

STEP 4

Fry the marinated squid on medium heat until golden and slightly crispy. Avoid overcooking.

STEP 5

Serve hot as a starter or side with rice and curry.

Ingredients

  • 250g squid (kalanji), cleaned and sliced
  • 1 tsp chili powder
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1/2 tsp crushed black pepper
  • 1 tbsp garlic, crushed
  • 1 tsp lemon juice
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tbsp coconut oil
  • Curry leaves (a handful)
  • 1 small onion (optional), sliced

Tools

  • Mixing bowl
  • Heavy-bottomed frying pan
  • Slotted spoon
  • Strainer
  • Cutting board and knife
