Banana Chips

Golden, crispy, and addictive — Kerala-style banana chips made from raw nendran bananas, fried in coconut oil and lightly salted. A classic crunchy snack!

Prep Time: 10 mins
Cook Time: 20 mins
Servings: 4–6
Snack Kerala Vegetarian Crispy

Instructions

STEP 1

Peel raw nendran bananas and slice them thinly using a mandoline or slicer. Keep in water with a pinch of turmeric to prevent discoloration.

STEP 2

Heat coconut oil in a deep pan. Once hot, add a batch of drained banana slices carefully into the oil.

STEP 3

Fry on medium heat, stirring occasionally until golden and crisp. In the last minute, sprinkle salted turmeric water (optional) into the oil for extra flavor and color.

STEP 4

Remove and drain on paper towels. Cool completely before storing in an airtight container.

Ingredients

  • 4 raw nendran bananas
  • 500ml coconut oil (for frying)
  • 1/4 tsp turmeric powder
  • Salt to taste
  • Water as needed

Tools

  • Banana slicer or mandoline
  • Deep frying pan or kadai
  • Slotted spoon
  • Paper towels
  • Airtight storage container
