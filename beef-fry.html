Kerala Beef Fry

A spicy, smoky, and delicious dry beef roast that’s slow-cooked with coconut slices, black pepper, curry leaves, and traditional Kerala spices. A perfect sidekick to porotta or rice!

Prep Time: 20 mins
Cook Time: 50 mins
Servings: 4
Lunch Non-Vegetarian Kerala Spicy

Instructions

STEP 1

Clean beef and cut into small cubes. Pressure cook with salt, turmeric, chili powder, coriander powder, and garam masala for 3–4 whistles.

STEP 2

Heat coconut oil in a pan. Fry coconut slices until golden. Add crushed ginger, garlic, and shallots. Sauté until soft.

STEP 3

Add cooked beef and fry on medium heat. Stir continuously until it’s dry and dark brown. Garnish with curry leaves and serve hot.

Ingredients

  • 500g beef, cubed
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp chili powder
  • 1 tbsp coriander powder
  • 1/2 tsp garam masala
  • 10 shallots, sliced
  • 2 tbsp crushed ginger-garlic
  • 1/2 cup coconut slices
  • Curry leaves
  • Salt to taste
  • 3 tbsp coconut oil

Tools

  • Pressure cooker
  • Heavy-bottomed pan
  • Wooden spatula
  • Knife and chopping board
