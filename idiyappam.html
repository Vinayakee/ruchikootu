Idiyappam

Ingredients

  • Rice flour - 2 cups
  • Hot water - as needed
  • Salt - to taste
  • Grated coconut - 1/2 cup

Preparation Steps

  1. Mix rice flour with salt and pour hot water to make a soft dough.
  2. Use idiyappam press to make noodles onto idli plates.
  3. Sprinkle coconut and steam for 10 minutes.
  4. Serve with stew or curry.
