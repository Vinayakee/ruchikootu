Idiyappam (Noolputtu)

Soft, delicate, and light — Idiyappam is a traditional Kerala steamed rice noodle often served with coconut milk, stew, or curry. A breakfast favorite that feels like home.

Prep Time: 25 mins
Cook Time: 15 mins
Servings: 3–4
Breakfast Vegetarian Steamed Kerala

Instructions

STEP 1

Boil 1.5 cups of water with a pinch of salt. In a mixing bowl, add roasted rice flour and gradually pour in the hot water while mixing.

STEP 2

Once it cools slightly, knead into a soft, non-sticky dough. Cover with a damp cloth and rest for 10 minutes.

STEP 3

Grease an idiyappam press and fill it with dough. Press the dough into thin noodles directly onto greased idli plates or banana leaves.

STEP 4

Sprinkle some grated coconut on top (optional) and steam in an idli steamer for 10–12 minutes.

STEP 5

Serve hot with coconut milk, vegetable stew, or egg curry.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups roasted rice flour (idiyappam podi)
  • 1.5 cups water (hot)
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 tsp coconut oil (optional)
  • Grated coconut (optional, for topping)

Tools

  • Idiyappam press (sevanazhi)
  • Steamer or idli cooker
  • Mixing bowl and spoon
  • Measuring cups/spoons
