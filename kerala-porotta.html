Kerala Porotta

Flaky, layered flatbread that’s a signature street food in Kerala. Soft inside and crisp outside, it pairs perfectly with spicy curries or just some curd and pickle.

Prep Time: 2 hrs (including resting)
Cook Time: 30 mins
Servings: 4–6
Bread Kerala Street Food Popular

Instructions

STEP 1

In a bowl, combine maida, sugar, salt, and a little oil. Add water gradually and knead into a soft dough. Let it rest for 1–2 hours.

STEP 2

Divide dough into small balls. Roll each into a thin sheet, brush with oil, and fold into a pleated spiral. Flatten gently.

STEP 3

Heat a tawa. Cook each porotta on both sides with a little oil until golden and flaky.

STEP 4

Stack cooked porottas and gently crush them using both hands for fluffiness. Serve hot with your favorite curry.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups maida (all-purpose flour)
  • 1/2 tsp sugar
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 2 tbsp oil (for dough)
  • Water as needed
  • Oil for cooking

Tools

  • Mixing bowl
  • Rolling pin
  • Tawa or flat pan
  • Spatula
  • Clean surface for rolling
