Kanji Payar

A comforting Kerala dish made with rice gruel (kanji) and green gram curry (payar), best enjoyed with pickle and papadam. Simple, nourishing, and satisfying.

Prep Time: 15 mins
Cook Time: 30 mins
Servings: 3-4
Comfort Food Kerala Vegetarian Dinner

Instructions

STEP 1

Wash and pressure cook green gram (cherupayar) with water, turmeric, and salt until soft. Keep aside.

STEP 2

For Kanji: Cook rice with lots of water and salt until it becomes soft and soupy.

STEP 3

In a pan, heat coconut oil and splutter mustard seeds. Add curry leaves, dry red chilies, chopped onions, and sauté.

STEP 4

Add the cooked green gram to the sautéed mix. Simmer for 5–10 minutes. Serve hot with kanji, pickle, and papadam.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup rice (for kanji)
  • 4 cups water (or more as needed)
  • 1 cup green gram (cherupayar)
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 tbsp coconut oil
  • 1/2 tsp mustard seeds
  • 1 sprig curry leaves
  • 2 dry red chilies
  • 1 small onion, chopped

Tools

  • Pressure cooker
  • Cooking pot
  • Spatula
  • Ladle
