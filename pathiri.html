Pathiri

Soft, thin rice pancakes made with roasted rice flour, traditionally served in Malabar cuisine. A must-have with spicy meat or chicken curry.

Prep Time: 15 mins
Cook Time: 15 mins
Servings: 4
Breakfast Kerala Traditional Gluten-free

Instructions

STEP 1

Boil water with a pinch of salt. Lower the heat and slowly add rice flour while stirring continuously to avoid lumps.

STEP 2

When the dough comes together, switch off the heat. Cover and let it rest for 5 minutes. Knead into a soft dough while warm.

STEP 3

Make small balls from the dough. Roll each ball into a thin circle using a rolling pin or pathiri press. Use rice flour to avoid sticking.

STEP 4

Heat a tawa. Cook each pathiri on both sides for a minute. Optionally, press gently with a spatula to puff it up. Do not brown.

STEP 5

Stack and keep covered in a hot box or container lined with cloth to keep soft. Serve hot with curry.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups roasted rice flour (pathiri podi)
  • 2 cups water (approx.)
  • Salt to taste

Tools

  • Mixing bowl
  • Rolling pin or pathiri press
  • Flat tawa or griddle
  • Spatula
  • Hot box or clean cloth
