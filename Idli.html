Idli

Soft and fluffy South Indian idlis made from fermented rice and urad dal batter. A classic breakfast staple served with sambar and chutney.

Prep Time: 10 mins
Cook Time: 15 mins
Servings: 4
Breakfast South Indian Vegetarian Steamed

Instructions

STEP 1

Soak rice and urad dal separately for 4–6 hours. Add a few fenugreek seeds to the dal while soaking.

STEP 2

Grind dal to a smooth batter and rice to a slightly coarse batter. Mix both together, add salt, and let it ferment overnight.

STEP 3

Pour the fermented batter into greased idli molds and steam for 10–12 minutes.

STEP 4

Remove and serve hot with coconut chutney and sambar.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups idli rice
  • 1 cup urad dal
  • 1/2 tsp fenugreek seeds
  • Salt to taste
  • Water as needed

Tools

  • Idli steamer or pressure cooker
  • Idli molds/plates
  • Mixer grinder
  • Large bowl (for fermentation)
