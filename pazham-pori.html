Pazham Pori

A beloved Kerala tea-time snack made from ripe bananas dipped in a lightly sweetened flour batter and deep-fried to golden perfection.

Prep Time: 10 mins
Cook Time: 15 mins
Servings: 4
Snack Tea Time Kerala Vegetarian

Instructions

STEP 1

Peel and slice ripe bananas (nendran) lengthwise into thin strips.

STEP 2

In a bowl, mix all-purpose flour, rice flour, sugar, turmeric, and salt with water to make a thick, smooth batter.

STEP 3

Dip banana slices in the batter, coating them well.

STEP 4

Heat oil in a deep pan. Fry the coated banana slices until golden brown on both sides. Drain on paper towels.

STEP 5

Serve hot with chai or as a sweet snack anytime!

Ingredients

  • 2 ripe nendran bananas
  • 1/2 cup all-purpose flour (maida)
  • 2 tbsp rice flour
  • 2 tbsp sugar (adjust to taste)
  • A pinch of turmeric powder
  • A pinch of salt
  • Water as needed
  • Oil for deep frying

Tools

  • Mixing bowl
  • Whisk or spoon
  • Deep frying pan
  • Slotted spoon
  • Paper towels
