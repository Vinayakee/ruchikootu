Pazham Pori

Ingredients

  • Ripe plantains - 2
  • All-purpose flour - 1 cup
  • Rice flour - 2 tbsp
  • Sugar - 2 tbsp
  • Turmeric powder - 1/4 tsp
  • Water - as needed
  • Oil - for deep frying

Preparation Steps

  1. Peel and slice the plantains lengthwise.
  2. Make a smooth batter with flour, rice flour, sugar, turmeric, and water.
  3. Dip the slices in batter and deep fry till golden brown.
  4. Serve hot with tea!
