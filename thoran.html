Thoran

A dry Kerala-style vegetable stir-fry made with coconut, mustard seeds, and curry leaves. It’s healthy, aromatic, and a staple in every Sadhya or daily meal.

Prep Time: 10 mins
Cook Time: 15 mins
Servings: 3-4
Vegetarian Kerala Healthy Lunch

Instructions

STEP 1

Finely chop or grate your chosen vegetable (cabbage, beans, carrot, or a mix). Wash and drain.

STEP 2

In a bowl, mix the vegetable with grated coconut, green chilies, turmeric, and salt. Set aside.

STEP 3

Heat coconut oil in a pan. Add mustard seeds and let them splutter. Add curry leaves and chopped shallots.

STEP 4

Add the vegetable mixture. Stir gently and cook covered on low heat for 7–10 mins. Stir occasionally.

STEP 5

Once cooked but not mushy, remove lid and stir-fry on high for a minute. Serve hot with rice.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups finely chopped cabbage/beans/carrot mix
  • 1/2 cup grated coconut
  • 2 green chilies, slit
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • Salt to taste
  • 1/2 tsp mustard seeds
  • 1 sprig curry leaves
  • 2 tbsp coconut oil
  • 4 shallots, chopped

Tools

  • Cutting board
  • Knife
  • Mixing bowl
  • Frying pan with lid
  • Spatula
