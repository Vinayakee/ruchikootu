Fluffy and sweet banana pancakes perfect for a cozy breakfast or brunch! Topped with caramel, berries, and a hint of cinnamon, this recipe is simple, healthy, and utterly delicious.

Prep Time: 30 mins
Cook Time: 15 mins
Servings: 6
Breakfast Pancakes Vegetarian Sweet

Instructions

STEP 1

Mash bananas in a large bowl. Add eggs, milk, and vanilla extract. Whisk until smooth.

STEP 2

Add flour, flaxseed, baking powder, and salt. Mix gently to form batter.

STEP 3

Heat nonstick skillet over medium. Pour batter, cook until bubbles form, flip and cook until golden.

Ingredients

  • 1 ½ cups pancake mix
  • ½ cup flaxseed meal
  • 1 cup skim milk
  • 2 ripe bananas, mashed
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • Pinch of salt

Tools

  • Nonstick pan or griddle
  • Mixing bowls
  • Spatula
  • Whisk
