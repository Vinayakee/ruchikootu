Kerala Sadya

Ingredients

  • Rice - 2 cups
  • Parippu Curry (dal curry) - 1 bowl
  • Avial - 1 bowl
  • Thoran (vegetable stir-fry) - 1 bowl
  • Pickles - 1 jar
  • Appalam (papadam) - 2 pieces
  • Rasam - 1 bowl
  • Buttermilk - 1 bowl
  • Banana - 1

Preparation Steps

  1. Prepare each dish (dal curry, avial, thoran, rasam) separately.
  2. Serve rice first, then add all sides and accompaniments.
  3. Eat with banana leaf and enjoy the rich flavors!
