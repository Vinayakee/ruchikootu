Chicken Roast

Kerala-style chicken roast — juicy chicken cooked in rich masala until dry and perfectly caramelized. A festive favorite with a spicy, bold twist.

Prep Time: 30 mins
Cook Time: 40 mins
Servings: 4
Spicy Kerala Non-Vegetarian Lunch

Instructions

STEP 1

Marinate chicken with turmeric, red chili powder, coriander powder, salt, and lemon juice. Let it sit for 30 minutes.

STEP 2

Heat coconut oil in a pan, shallow fry the marinated chicken pieces until browned and cooked through. Remove and keep aside.

STEP 3

In the same oil, sauté onions, green chilies, curry leaves, ginger, and garlic until golden brown.

STEP 4

Add remaining spices and a splash of water. Cook until oil separates. Add the fried chicken and roast on low heat until masala coats well.

Ingredients

  • 750g chicken, cut into medium pieces
  • 1 tsp turmeric powder
  • 2 tsp red chili powder
  • 1 tbsp coriander powder
  • 1/2 tsp garam masala
  • 1 tsp black pepper powder
  • 2 onions, thinly sliced
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 2 green chilies, slit
  • 1 sprig curry leaves
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • Salt to taste
  • 4 tbsp coconut oil

Tools

  • Heavy-bottomed pan
  • Mixing bowl
  • Spatula
  • Knife & cutting board
