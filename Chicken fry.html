Chicken Fry

Kerala-style chicken fry — crispy, spicy and deep-fried to perfection. A must-try traditional favorite served with rice or parotta.

Prep Time: 25 mins
Cook Time: 20 mins
Servings: 4
Spicy Kerala Non-Vegetarian Starter

Instructions

STEP 1

Clean and cut the chicken into small pieces. Marinate with turmeric, chili powder, garam masala, ginger-garlic paste, lemon juice, and salt. Rest for 1 hour.

STEP 2

Heat oil in a deep pan. Add curry leaves and marinated chicken pieces in batches.

STEP 3

Fry until crispy and golden brown on the outside and cooked inside. Drain on paper towels.

STEP 4

Garnish with fried curry leaves and serve hot with sliced onions and lemon.

Ingredients

  • 500g chicken, cut into small pieces
  • 1 tsp turmeric powder
  • 2 tsp red chili powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1 sprig curry leaves
  • Salt to taste
  • Oil for deep frying

Tools

  • Mixing bowl
  • Deep frying pan
  • Slotted spoon
  • Paper towels
