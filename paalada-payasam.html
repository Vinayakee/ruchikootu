Paalada Payasam

A classic Kerala dessert made with palada (rice flakes), milk, and sugar. This sweet and creamy delight is often prepared during festivals and special occasions.

Prep Time: 10 mins
Cook Time: 1 hour
Servings: 6
Dessert Vegetarian Kerala Festive

Instructions

STEP 1

Boil full-fat milk in a thick-bottomed vessel. Keep stirring occasionally to avoid burning at the bottom.

STEP 2

Rinse palada flakes in warm water and drain. Add to boiling milk and cook on low flame until soft.

STEP 3

Add sugar and continue stirring. Let the payasam thicken and develop a light pinkish color.

STEP 4

Once the desired consistency is reached, turn off the flame. Serve hot or chilled.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup palada (rice flakes for payasam)
  • 1.5 liters full-fat milk
  • 1 cup sugar (adjust to taste)

Tools

  • Thick-bottomed pan
  • Wooden spatula
  • Strainer
  • Measuring cups
