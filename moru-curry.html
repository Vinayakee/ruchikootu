Moru Curry (Pulisseri)

A cooling, tangy buttermilk curry spiced with coconut and aromatic tempering — a must-have comfort dish in every Kerala meal!

Prep Time: 10 mins
Cook Time: 15 mins
Servings: 4
Vegetarian Kerala Traditional Cool

Instructions

STEP 1

Grind grated coconut, green chilies, cumin seeds, and a little water into a smooth paste.

STEP 2

In a pan, add turmeric powder and the coconut paste. Cook for a couple of minutes on low flame without boiling.

STEP 3

Lower the flame and gently stir in the whisked curd or buttermilk. Heat gently without allowing it to curdle. Add salt to taste.

STEP 4

In a small pan, heat coconut oil. Splutter mustard seeds, fenugreek seeds, dry red chilies, curry leaves, and chopped shallots. Pour the tempering into the curry.

STEP 5

Serve warm with steamed rice and a spicy side dish like thoran or mezhukkupuratti.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups sour curd or buttermilk
  • 1/2 cup grated coconut
  • 2 green chilies
  • 1/2 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1/4 tsp turmeric powder
  • Salt to taste
  • Water as needed
  • 1 tbsp coconut oil
  • 1/2 tsp mustard seeds
  • 1/4 tsp fenugreek seeds
  • 2 dry red chilies
  • 1 sprig curry leaves
  • 2 shallots, sliced (optional)

Tools

  • Blender or mixie
  • Clay pot or deep pan
  • Tempering pan
  • Spatula
  • Ladle
