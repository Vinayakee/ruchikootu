Moru Curry

Ingredients

  • To make Coconut Paste
  • Freshly grated coconut - 1/2 cup
  • Green chillies(roughly chopped) - 2
  • Water - as needed to blend
  • To make Curd Mixture
  • Sour Curd - 1 Cup
  • Water - 1 cup
  • Turmeric powder - 1/4 tsp
  • Salt - to taste
  • For Tempering
  • Oil - 2 tbsp
  • Dried red chillies - 2
  • Mustard seeds - 1 tsp
  • Ginger(finely chopped) - 1/4 tsp
  • Onion(chopped) - 1/2
  • Curry leaves - few
  • For Garnishing (optional)
  • Fresh coriander leaves(finley chopped) - 2-3

Preparation Steps

  1. Blend grated coconut, green chillies, cumin seeds, and water into a smooth paste.
  2. Whisk curd with water and turmeric to a buttermilk consistency, then mix in the coconut paste and salt.
  3. Heat the mixture on low without overboiling to prevent curdling.
  4. In oil, crackle mustard seeds, then saut√© curry leaves, methi seeds, ginger, dry red chillies, and onions.
  5. Pour the tempering over the curry, cover to infuse flavors, and garnish with coriander leaves before serving.
