Pepper Roast Chicken

Fiery and aromatic Pepper Roast Chicken — a Kerala-style delicacy loaded with black pepper, spices, and slow-roasted masala. Perfect with chapati or rice!

Prep Time: 25 mins
Cook Time: 35 mins
Servings: 4
Spicy Kerala Non-Vegetarian Dinner

Instructions

STEP 1

Marinate chicken with turmeric, salt, lemon juice, and crushed black pepper. Let it rest for 30 minutes.

STEP 2

Heat oil in a pan, sauté onions, green chilies, curry leaves, and ginger-garlic paste until golden.

STEP 3

Add coriander powder, garam masala, and freshly ground black pepper. Sauté for a few seconds.

STEP 4

Add marinated chicken and cook on medium heat until tender. Roast until masala coats and turns dark.

Ingredients

  • 500g chicken (bone-in or boneless)
  • 2 onions, thinly sliced
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 2 green chilies, slit
  • 1 tsp turmeric powder
  • 2 tsp crushed black pepper
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1 tsp coriander powder
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1 sprig curry leaves
  • Salt to taste
  • 3 tbsp coconut oil

Tools

  • Deep frying pan
  • Wooden spatula
  • Mixing bowl
  • Chopping board & knife
