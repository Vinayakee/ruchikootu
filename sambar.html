Sambar

Ingredients

  • Toor dal (pigeon peas) - 1 cup
  • Mixed vegetables (Carrot, Potato, Pumpkin, Drumstick) - 2 cups
  • Tomato - 1, chopped
  • Onion - 1, sliced
  • Green chilies - 2, slit
  • Sambar powder - 1 tbsp
  • Tamarind extract - 1 tbsp
  • Curry leaves - a handful
  • Coconut oil - 2 tbsp
  • Salt - to taste

Preparation Steps

  1. Cook the toor dal until soft and mash it.
  2. In a separate pan, saut√© onions, tomatoes, and green chilies. Add mixed vegetables and cook until tender.
  3. Add tamarind extract, sambar powder, and salt. Let it simmer for 10 minutes.
  4. Once the vegetables are cooked, add the mashed dal and adjust consistency with water.
  5. Heat coconut oil in a pan and fry curry leaves. Add it to the sambar.
  6. Serve hot with rice or dosa.
