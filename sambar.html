Sambar

A wholesome South Indian lentil and vegetable stew flavored with tamarind and aromatic spices. A must-have in every Kerala Sadhya and perfect with rice or dosa.

Prep Time: 15 mins
Cook Time: 30 mins
Servings: 4–5
Lunch Vegetarian Kerala Healthy

Instructions

STEP 1

Pressure cook toor dal with turmeric and water until soft. Mash and set aside.

STEP 2

In a pot, cook vegetables (drumstick, carrots, beans, etc.) in tamarind water with salt and turmeric until soft.

STEP 3

Add cooked dal to the vegetables. Mix in sambar powder and simmer for 5–7 mins.

STEP 4

In a pan, heat coconut oil. Splutter mustard seeds, add dried red chilies, curry leaves, and hing. Pour over sambar and mix well.

STEP 5

Garnish with fresh coriander and serve hot with rice or dosa.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup toor dal
  • 1/4 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 lemon-sized tamarind soaked in water
  • 1.5 cups mixed vegetables (carrot, drumstick, brinjal, beans, etc.)
  • 2–3 tbsp sambar powder
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tsp coconut oil
  • 1/2 tsp mustard seeds
  • 2 dried red chilies
  • 1 sprig curry leaves
  • A pinch of hing (asafoetida)
  • Fresh coriander for garnish

Tools

  • Pressure cooker
  • Large pot or kadai
  • Tempering pan
  • Measuring spoons
  • Ladle and spatula
