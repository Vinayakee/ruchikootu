Neer Dosa

A soft, lacy dosa made with just rice and water — a Mangalorean delicacy that pairs beautifully with chutneys or spicy curries.

Prep Time: 4 hours (soaking)
Cook Time: 15 mins
Servings: 3
Breakfast Vegan Gluten-Free South Indian

Instructions

STEP 1

Wash and soak raw rice in water for at least 4 hours or overnight.

STEP 2

Drain the soaked rice and grind it with water and a pinch of salt into a smooth, watery batter.

STEP 3

The batter should be very thin (like buttermilk). Adjust with more water if needed.

STEP 4

Heat a non-stick pan. Pour a ladle of batter and swirl the pan so it spreads thin. Do not spread with the spoon.

STEP 5

Cover and cook on medium heat for a minute. No need to flip. Remove gently and serve hot with chutney or curry.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup raw rice (sona masoori or dosa rice)
  • Salt to taste
  • Water (as needed)
  • Oil (for greasing the pan)

Tools

  • Blender or wet grinder
  • Non-stick dosa tawa or pan
  • Ladle
  • Mixing bowl
  • Measuring cups
