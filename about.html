About Us – Ruchikootu

Welcome to Ruchikootu – Where Flavors Meet Stories!

At Ruchikootu, food isn’t just a recipe — it’s a celebration of tradition, creativity, and community. We’re a passionate team of food lovers, home cooks, and tech enthusiasts bringing together the best of both worlds: delicious, home-style cooking and smart technology.

🍲 Our Mission

To create a vibrant, inclusive, and interactive platform where anyone — from a beginner to a seasoned chef — can explore, share, and fall in love with food.

📚 What You’ll Find on Ruchikootu

  • Curated Recipes: Handpicked dishes with clear steps and user ratings.
  • Interactive Cooking Games: Fun recipe-based games to test your skills and speed.
  • User Ratings & Reviews: Share feedback and learn from others’ experiences.
  • Smart Search & Filters: Find recipes by cuisine, ingredients, or cooking time.
  • Community Features: Profiles, challenges, and recipe submissions (Coming Soon).

🧑‍💻 Built by Students, Powered by Passion

Ruchikootu is proudly developed as a student project using Java Swing for frontend and MySQL for backend.

💬 Why ‘Ruchikootu’?

‘Ruchikootu’ means “a gathering of taste” in Malayalam — our tribute to Kerala’s rich and diverse food culture.
