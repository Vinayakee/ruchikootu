Chicken Curry

This Kerala-style chicken curry is a rich, spicy, and aromatic dish that combines coconut milk, curry leaves, and traditional spices. Perfect with rice or parotta!

Prep Time: 20 mins
Cook Time: 40 mins
Servings: 4
Lunch Non-Vegetarian Kerala Spicy

Instructions

STEP 1

Clean and cut chicken into medium pieces. Marinate with turmeric, salt, and a bit of chili powder. Set aside for 15 mins.

STEP 2

Heat oil in a pan, sauté onions until golden brown. Add ginger-garlic paste and sauté until raw smell disappears.

STEP 3

Add chopped tomatoes and cook until soft. Then add coriander, chili, and garam masala powders. Cook until oil separates.

STEP 4

Add chicken pieces and mix well with masala. Cook covered for 10 mins on low flame.

STEP 5

Pour coconut milk and bring to a gentle boil. Simmer until chicken is fully cooked and curry thickens. Garnish with curry leaves.

Ingredients

  • 500g chicken, cleaned and cut
  • 2 onions, finely sliced
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tsp turmeric powder
  • 2 tsp chili powder
  • 2 tsp coriander powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1 cup thick coconut milk
  • Curry leaves
  • Salt to taste
  • 3 tbsp coconut oil

Tools

  • Heavy-bottomed pan or kadai
  • Wooden spoon or spatula
  • Knife and chopping board
  • Measuring cups/spoons
