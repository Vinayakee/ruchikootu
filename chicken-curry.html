Chicken Curry

Ingredients

  • Chicken - 500g, cut into pieces
  • Onion - 2, chopped
  • Tomato - 1, chopped
  • Ginger-garlic paste - 1 tbsp
  • Chili powder - 2 tsp
  • Turmeric - 1/2 tsp
  • Coriander powder - 1 tsp
  • Garam masala - 1 tsp
  • Coconut oil - 2 tbsp
  • Salt - to taste
  • Curry leaves - few

Preparation Steps

  1. Heat oil, saut√© onions, ginger-garlic paste, and tomatoes.
  2. Add chicken and cook with spices till tender.
  3. Finish with curry leaves. Serve hot with rice!
