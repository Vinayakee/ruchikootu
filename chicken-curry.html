Fish Curry

Ingredients

  • Fish (any firm fish) - 500g
  • Onion - 1, sliced
  • Tomato - 1, chopped
  • Ginger-garlic paste - 1 tbsp
  • Chili powder - 2 tsp
  • Turmeric - 1/4 tsp
  • Curry leaves - few
  • Coconut oil - 2 tbsp
  • Salt - to taste

Preparation Steps

  1. Heat oil, saut√© onions, ginger-garlic, and tomatoes.
  2. Add chicken, season with spices, and cook in coconut milk or water.
  3. Simmer and garnish with curry leaves. Serve hot!
