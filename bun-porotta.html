Bun Porotta

Fluffy, layered, and soft as a cloud, Kerala Bun Porotta is a perfect blend of crisp edges and tender insides. Pair it with spicy curry for the ultimate comfort meal.

Prep Time: 1 hr
Cook Time: 20 mins
Servings: 3-4
Dinner Kerala Wheat Street Food

Instructions

STEP 1

In a large bowl, mix all-purpose flour, sugar, salt, curd, and baking soda. Add water gradually and knead to a soft dough.

STEP 2

Apply oil on top of the dough, cover with a damp cloth and rest it for at least 1 hour.

STEP 3

Divide into equal balls and roll each one into thick circular discs. Allow them to rest again for 10 minutes.

STEP 4

Heat a tawa, cook each side until golden brown, flipping and pressing to puff up slightly. Serve hot!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour (maida)
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 2 tbsp curd
  • 1/4 tsp baking soda
  • Water as needed
  • Oil for kneading and cooking

Tools

  • Mixing bowl
  • Tawa or skillet
  • Rolling pin
  • Cloth for covering
