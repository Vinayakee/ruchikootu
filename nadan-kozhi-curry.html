Nadan Kozhi Curry

A rich and flavorful Kerala-style chicken curry made with roasted spices and coconut. Perfect with rice, appam, or parotta!

Prep Time: 20 mins
Cook Time: 40 mins
Servings: 5
Non-Vegetarian Kerala Spicy Traditional

Instructions

STEP 1

Clean and cut the chicken into medium-sized pieces. Marinate with salt, turmeric, and a bit of chili powder. Set aside for 15 mins.

STEP 2

In a pan, dry roast grated coconut, fennel seeds, coriander seeds, cloves, cinnamon, and peppercorns until golden. Grind into a fine paste once cooled.

STEP 3

Heat coconut oil, sauté onions till golden brown. Add ginger, garlic, green chilies, and curry leaves. Sauté until raw smell goes.

STEP 4

Add chopped tomatoes and cook until soft. Then add chili powder, turmeric, and coriander powder. Cook until oil separates.

STEP 5

Add marinated chicken and mix well. Cook for 5 minutes, then add the ground coconut masala and enough water. Cover and cook until chicken is tender.

STEP 6

Garnish with curry leaves and a spoon of coconut oil. Serve hot with rice, chapati, appam, or Kerala parotta.

Ingredients

  • 750g chicken (curry cut)
  • 2 onions, sliced
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 2 green chilies
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tbsp chili powder
  • 1.5 tbsp coriander powder
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tbsp coconut oil
  • Curry leaves

For Roasted Coconut Masala

  • 1/2 cup grated coconut
  • 1/2 tsp fennel seeds
  • 1 tbsp coriander seeds
  • 3-4 cloves
  • 1-inch cinnamon stick
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper

Tools

  • Heavy-bottomed pan or clay pot
  • Spatula
  • Blender/grinder
  • Measuring cups/spoons
  • Lid for slow cooking
