Nadan Kozhi Curry

Ingredients

  • Chicken - 500g
  • Onion - 2, sliced
  • Tomato - 1, chopped
  • Ginger-garlic paste - 1 tbsp
  • Chili powder - 2 tsp
  • Coriander powder - 2 tsp
  • Turmeric - 1/2 tsp
  • Garam masala - 1 tsp
  • Coconut oil - 2 tbsp
  • Curry leaves - few
  • Salt - to taste

Preparation Steps

  1. Heat oil, saut√© onions and ginger-garlic.
  2. Add tomatoes and all powders.
  3. Add chicken, mix well, cook till done.
  4. Sprinkle garam masala and curry leaves before serving.
