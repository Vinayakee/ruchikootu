Chicken Kondattam

A fiery and tangy Kerala-style chicken dish with bold flavors. Chicken Kondattam is perfect as a side dish or as a spicy snack for festive meals.

Prep Time: 20 mins
Cook Time: 30 mins
Servings: 4
Spicy Kerala Non-Vegetarian Snack

Instructions

STEP 1

Marinate chicken with turmeric, chili powder, coriander powder, pepper, salt, and lemon juice. Let it sit for 30 minutes.

STEP 2

Deep fry the chicken pieces until golden brown and crispy. Set aside.

STEP 3

In another pan, heat oil and sauté onions, garlic, and curry leaves until fragrant. Add tomato ketchup, chili sauce, and soy sauce. Stir well.

STEP 4

Add the fried chicken pieces into the sauce and toss until well coated. Cook for 5 minutes on low flame. Garnish and serve hot.

Ingredients

  • 500g chicken, boneless or bone-in
  • 1 tsp turmeric powder
  • 2 tsp red chili powder
  • 1 tsp coriander powder
  • 1 tsp black pepper
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • 2 tbsp tomato ketchup
  • 1 tbsp red chili sauce
  • 1 tsp soy sauce
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 tbsp garlic, chopped
  • 1 sprig curry leaves
  • Salt to taste
  • Oil for frying

Tools

  • Mixing bowl
  • Frying pan
  • Spatula
  • Paper towels
