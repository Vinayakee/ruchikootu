Dosa

Golden, crispy South Indian dosa made from a fermented batter of rice and urad dal. Perfectly paired with coconut chutney and hot sambar.

Prep Time: 10 mins
Cook Time: 15 mins
Servings: 4
Breakfast South Indian Vegetarian Crispy

Instructions

STEP 1

Soak rice and urad dal separately for 4–6 hours. Add fenugreek seeds to the dal while soaking.

STEP 2

Grind dal to a fluffy paste and rice to a slightly coarse batter. Combine and mix well with salt. Ferment overnight.

STEP 3

Heat a non-stick tawa, pour a ladle of batter, and spread in a circular motion. Drizzle ghee or oil.

STEP 4

Cook until golden and crispy. Serve with sambar and chutney.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups rice
  • 1/2 cup urad dal
  • 1/2 tsp fenugreek seeds
  • Salt to taste
  • Oil or ghee for cooking

Tools

  • Flat non-stick tawa or iron griddle
  • Ladle for spreading
  • Mixer grinder
  • Fermentation bowl
