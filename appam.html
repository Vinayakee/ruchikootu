Appam

Soft in the center, crisp at the edges—Appam is a beloved Kerala breakfast made with fermented rice and coconut batter. Best enjoyed with stew or curry!

Prep Time: 15 mins
Cook Time: 20 mins
Servings: 4
Breakfast Vegetarian Kerala Classic

Instructions

STEP 1

Soak raw rice for 4-5 hours. Grind with grated coconut and cooked rice into a smooth batter using water as needed.

STEP 2

Add yeast, sugar, and salt. Mix well and allow the batter to ferment overnight or for 8 hours.

STEP 3

Once fermented, stir the batter gently. Adjust consistency if too thick using a little water.

STEP 4

Heat an appachatti (appam pan), pour a ladle of batter, swirl to spread, and cover. Cook until edges crisp and center is soft.

STEP 5

Serve hot with vegetable stew, egg curry, or fish molee!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups raw rice (soaked)
  • 1/2 cup cooked rice
  • 1 cup grated coconut
  • 1/2 tsp dry yeast
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • Salt to taste
  • Water as required

Tools

  • Grinder or blender
  • Appachatti (appam pan)
  • Mixing bowls
  • Ladle
