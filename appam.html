Appam

Ingredients

  • Raw rice - 2 cups
  • Cooked rice - 1/2 cup
  • Grated coconut - 1/2 cup
  • Yeast - 1/2 tsp
  • Sugar - 1 tbsp
  • Salt - to taste
  • Water - as needed

Preparation Steps

  1. Soak raw rice for 4 hours.
  2. Grind with coconut, cooked rice, and water to a smooth batter.
  3. Mix yeast and sugar, ferment for 6-8 hours.
  4. Add salt and make soft appams in appachatti.
