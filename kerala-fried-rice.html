Kerala Fried Rice

Ingredients

  • Cooked rice - 2 cups
  • Onion - 1, chopped
  • Ginger-garlic paste - 1 tbsp
  • Carrot - 1, grated
  • Beans - 1/4 cup, chopped
  • Capsicum - 1, chopped
  • Green chilies - 2, sliced
  • Curry leaves - few
  • Coconut oil - 2 tbsp
  • Salt - to taste

Preparation Steps

  1. Heat oil, saut√© onions, ginger-garlic paste, and vegetables.
  2. Add cooked rice and toss everything together.
  3. Season with salt and curry leaves. Serve hot!
