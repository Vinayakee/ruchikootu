Kerala Fried Rice

This flavorful fried rice combines the richness of ghee, subtle spices, and locally loved vegetables. A perfect festive or Sunday lunch treat from the kitchens of Kerala.

Prep Time: 20 mins
Cook Time: 20 mins
Servings: 4
Lunch Vegetarian Kerala Flavored Rice

Instructions

STEP 1

Cook basmati rice until fluffy but not mushy. Spread to cool and prevent sticking.

STEP 2

In a large pan, heat ghee. Sauté chopped onions, green chilies, and ginger-garlic paste until fragrant.

STEP 3

Add chopped vegetables like beans, carrots, and capsicum. Cook till just soft. Season with pepper, garam masala, and salt.

STEP 4

Mix in the rice and toss well with the veggies. Finish with fried cashews and raisins. Serve hot with raitha or curry.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups cooked basmati rice
  • 1 onion, finely sliced
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 2 green chilies, slit
  • 1/4 cup chopped carrots
  • 1/4 cup chopped beans
  • 1/4 cup capsicum
  • 1/4 tsp black pepper powder
  • 1/2 tsp garam masala
  • 2 tbsp ghee
  • 2 tbsp cashew nuts
  • 1 tbsp raisins
  • Salt to taste

Tools

  • Non-stick frying pan or wok
  • Rice cooker or pressure cooker
  • Wooden spatula
  • Chopping board & knife
