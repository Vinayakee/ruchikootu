Kalan Curry

Ingredients

  • Yam (chena) - 1 cup, diced
  • Raw banana - 1, diced
  • Curd - 1 cup
  • Grated coconut - 1/2 cup
  • Green chilies - 2
  • Black pepper powder - 1 tsp
  • Cumin seeds - 1 tsp
  • Coconut oil - 1 tbsp
  • Mustard seeds - 1/2 tsp
  • Curry leaves - few
  • Salt - to taste

Preparation Steps

  1. Cook yam and banana with salt and pepper.
  2. Grind coconut, cumin, chilies to a paste.
  3. Add curd and coconut paste to cooked veggies.
  4. Temper mustard seeds and curry leaves in oil and pour over.
