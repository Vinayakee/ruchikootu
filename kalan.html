Kalan

A traditional Kerala dish made with raw banana and thick yogurt, delicately spiced with black pepper and coconut. A must-have in every Kerala Sadhya!

Prep Time: 15 mins
Cook Time: 25 mins
Servings: 4
Vegetarian Kerala Sadhya Festive

Instructions

STEP 1

Peel and cube the raw banana. Cook it with turmeric, black pepper, salt, and water until tender.

STEP 2

Grind grated coconut, green chilies, and cumin seeds into a fine paste using a little water.

STEP 3

Add the coconut paste to the cooked banana. Simmer gently for 5‚Äì7 minutes.

STEP 4

Lower the flame and add whisked curd. Stir continuously until slightly thickened. Do not boil.

STEP 5

Heat coconut oil, splutter mustard seeds, add dried red chilies and curry leaves. Pour the tempering over the kalan. Serve warm with rice.

Ingredients

  • 2 raw bananas (nendran or regular), peeled and cubed
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper powder
  • 1 cup thick curd (yogurt), whisked
  • Salt to taste
  • Water as needed
  • 1 tbsp coconut oil
  • 1/2 tsp mustard seeds
  • 2 dried red chilies
  • Curry leaves
