Avial

Ingredients

  • Mixed vegetables - 2 cups (carrot, beans, yam, raw banana)
  • Grated coconut - 1 cup
  • Green chilies - 2
  • Cumin seeds - 1 tsp
  • Coconut oil - 2 tbsp
  • Curry leaves - few
  • Curd - 1/2 cup (optional)
  • Salt - to taste

Preparation Steps

  1. Cook vegetables with salt and little water.
  2. Grind coconut, cumin, green chilies to coarse paste.
  3. Add paste and curd to veggies, mix gently.
  4. Drizzle coconut oil and curry leaves on top. Serve!
