Prawns Masala

A spicy and aromatic Kerala-style prawn masala that brings out the flavors of the coast — perfect with rice, chapathi, or appam.

Prep Time: 20 mins
Cook Time: 25 mins
Servings: 3–4
Seafood Kerala Spicy Lunch/Dinner

Instructions

STEP 1

Clean and devein prawns. Marinate with turmeric, chili powder, and salt for 15 minutes.

STEP 2

Heat coconut oil in a pan. Add mustard seeds and let them splutter. Add curry leaves, chopped onions, green chilies, and sauté until golden.

STEP 3

Add ginger-garlic paste and sauté until the raw smell goes. Add chili powder, coriander powder, and garam masala. Mix well.

STEP 4

Add tomatoes and cook until mushy. Add marinated prawns and mix gently. Cook for 8–10 minutes on medium heat until prawns are done.

STEP 5

Garnish with fresh coriander leaves and serve hot with rice or parotta.

Ingredients

  • 300g prawns, cleaned & deveined
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tbsp coconut oil
  • 1/2 tsp mustard seeds
  • Curry leaves
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 green chilies, slit
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tsp coriander powder
  • 1/2 tsp garam masala
  • 1 tomato, chopped
  • Fresh coriander for garnish

Tools

  • Mixing bowl
  • Non-stick pan or kadai
  • Spatula
  • Knife and chopping board
  • Serving dish
