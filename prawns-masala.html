Prawns Masala

Ingredients

  • Prawns - 500g, cleaned
  • Onion - 2, chopped
  • Tomato - 1, chopped
  • Ginger-garlic paste - 1 tbsp
  • Chili powder - 2 tsp
  • Turmeric - 1/4 tsp
  • Coriander powder - 1 tsp
  • Garam masala - 1 tsp
  • Coconut oil - 2 tbsp
  • Curry leaves - few
  • Salt - to taste

Preparation Steps

  1. Heat oil, saut√© onions, ginger-garlic paste.
  2. Add tomatoes and spices. Cook till soft.
  3. Mix in prawns and cook till tender.
  4. Finish with curry leaves. Serve hot!
