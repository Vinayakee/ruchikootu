Beef Roast

Kerala-style beef roast — tender beef slow-cooked in aromatic spices and roasted in coconut oil. A perfect side for porotta or rice!

Prep Time: 20 mins
Cook Time: 1 hr
Servings: 5
Kerala Non-Vegetarian Spicy Dinner

Instructions

STEP 1

Pressure cook beef with turmeric, chili powder, coriander, pepper, garam masala, salt, and a little water until tender.

STEP 2

In a pan, heat coconut oil. Add sliced onions, curry leaves, ginger, and garlic. Sauté until golden brown.

STEP 3

Add the cooked beef with stock. Roast on low flame until the masala becomes thick and beef is dark brown and dry.

STEP 4

Garnish with more curry leaves and a splash of coconut oil. Serve hot.

Ingredients

  • 500g beef, cubed
  • 1 tsp turmeric powder
  • 2 tsp red chili powder
  • 1 tbsp coriander powder
  • 1/2 tsp garam masala
  • 1 tsp black pepper powder
  • 2 onions, sliced
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • Curry leaves
  • Salt to taste
  • 3 tbsp coconut oil

Tools

  • Pressure cooker
  • Deep pan/kadai
  • Spatula
  • Knife & chopping board
