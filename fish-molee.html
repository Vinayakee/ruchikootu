Fish Molee

Home Back to Recipes

Ingredients:

  • Fish slices - 500g
  • Coconut milk - 1 cup
  • Green chilies - 4 (slit)
  • Ginger - 1 tbsp (chopped)
  • Garlic - 1 tbsp (chopped)
  • Turmeric powder - 1/2 tsp
  • Pepper - 1/2 tsp
  • Onions - 2 (sliced)
  • Curry leaves - few
  • Oil - 2 tbsp
  • Salt - as needed

Instructions:

  1. Marinate fish with salt, turmeric, and pepper. Set aside.
  2. In a pan, heat oil. Saut√© onions, ginger, garlic, green chilies, and curry leaves.
  3. Add marinated fish pieces and cook gently.
  4. Pour coconut milk and simmer for 5-7 minutes without boiling hard.
  5. Garnish with curry leaves. Serve hot with appam or rice.
