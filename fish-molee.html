Fish Molee

This creamy and mildly spiced Fish Molee is a traditional Kerala delicacy that blends seared fish with coconut milk, green chilies, and subtle spices. Perfect with appam or bread!

Prep Time: 15 mins
Cook Time: 30 mins
Servings: 3–4
Dinner Non-Vegetarian Kerala Mild

Instructions

STEP 1

Marinate fish with turmeric, pepper, and salt. Shallow fry lightly in coconut oil and set aside.

STEP 2

In the same pan, add sliced onions, green chilies, and ginger-garlic paste. Sauté until soft and translucent.

STEP 3

Add turmeric and lightly sauté. Pour in thin coconut milk and let it simmer for 5 minutes.

STEP 4

Add fried fish pieces gently and cook for another 8–10 minutes on low flame.

STEP 5

Pour thick coconut milk, adjust salt, and gently simmer for a couple of minutes. Garnish with curry leaves.

Ingredients

  • 500g seer fish or kingfish slices
  • 1 onion, thinly sliced
  • 3 green chilies, slit
  • 1 tsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 cup thin coconut milk
  • 1/2 cup thick coconut milk
  • Curry leaves
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 3 tbsp coconut oil

Tools

  • Flat pan or clay pot
  • Spatula
  • Knife and chopping board
  • Measuring spoons and cups
