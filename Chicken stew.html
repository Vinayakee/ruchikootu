Chicken Stew

Kerala-style Chicken Stew made with coconut milk, whole spices, and tender chicken – a mild and aromatic dish perfect for appam or idiyappam.

Prep Time: 20 mins
Cook Time: 30 mins
Servings: 4
Mild Kerala Non-Vegetarian Breakfast

Instructions

STEP 1

Marinate chicken with salt and pepper. Set aside for 15 minutes.

STEP 2

Heat coconut oil, sauté cinnamon, cardamom, cloves, and bay leaf. Add onions, ginger, garlic, and green chilies. Sauté until onions turn soft.

STEP 3

Add chicken pieces and cook until lightly browned. Add thin coconut milk and simmer until chicken is tender.

STEP 4

Stir in thick coconut milk, adjust salt, and simmer gently (do not boil). Garnish with curry leaves and serve warm.

Ingredients

  • 500g chicken (with bones or boneless)
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 1 tbsp ginger, chopped
  • 1 tbsp garlic, chopped
  • 2 green chilies, slit
  • 1 cinnamon stick
  • 3 cardamom pods
  • 3 cloves
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 1 cup thin coconut milk
  • 1/2 cup thick coconut milk
  • 1 sprig curry leaves
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 tbsp coconut oil

Tools

  • Deep cooking pan
  • Wooden spatula
  • Measuring cups
  • Knife and chopping board
