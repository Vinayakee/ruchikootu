Fish Pollichathu

Wrapped in banana leaves and grilled to perfection, this traditional Kerala delicacy is rich with coastal flavors and smoky aroma. Perfect for a special lunch or dinner!

Prep Time: 30 mins
Cook Time: 30 mins
Servings: 2
Dinner Kerala Seafood Banana Leaf

Instructions

STEP 1

Marinate cleaned fish (usually pearl spot or pomfret) with chili powder, turmeric, pepper, salt, and lemon juice. Set aside for 20 minutes.

STEP 2

In coconut oil, sauté chopped onions, green chilies, ginger-garlic paste, and curry leaves until golden brown. Add tomatoes and cook till mushy.

STEP 3

Add chili, turmeric, and coriander powders. Add thick coconut milk and simmer into a thick masala. Let it cool slightly.

STEP 4

Lightly fry the marinated fish until partially cooked. Place it over a banana leaf, spoon masala on top, and wrap securely.

STEP 5

Grill or roast the wrapped fish on a tawa/pan for 8–10 minutes each side. Serve hot with lemon wedges and steamed rice.

Ingredients

  • 2 pearl spot or pomfret fish
  • 1 tsp chili powder
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1/2 tsp pepper powder
  • Salt to taste
  • Juice of 1 lemon
  • 2 onions, finely chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 2 green chilies, sliced
  • 1 tsp coriander powder
  • 1/4 cup thick coconut milk
  • Curry leaves
  • 3 tbsp coconut oil
  • Banana leaves (lightly wilted over flame)

Tools

  • Flat tawa or skillet
  • Banana leaves
  • Kitchen twine or toothpicks
  • Mixing bowl
  • Spatula
