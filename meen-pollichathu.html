Meen Pollichathu

Ingredients

  • Fish fillet (Pearl Spot or Pomfret) - 2
  • Shallots - 10, sliced
  • Ginger-garlic paste - 1 tbsp
  • Chili powder - 1 tsp
  • Turmeric powder - 1/2 tsp
  • Coconut oil - 2 tbsp
  • Curry leaves - few
  • Banana leaves - for wrapping
  • Salt - to taste

Preparation Steps

  1. Marinate fish with salt, turmeric, and chili powder.
  2. Prepare masala by frying shallots, ginger-garlic, and spices.
  3. Wrap fish and masala in banana leaves and cook on a tawa till done.
  4. Serve hot with rice!
