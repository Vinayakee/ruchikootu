Egg Curry

Kerala-style egg curry in a luscious coconut-spiced gravy — a versatile and comforting dish that pairs beautifully with appam, puttu, or chapathi.

Prep Time: 15 mins
Cook Time: 25 mins
Servings: 4
Breakfast Vegetarian Kerala Curry

Instructions

STEP 1

Boil and peel the eggs. Slightly slit them and keep aside.

STEP 2

Heat coconut oil in a pan, sauté sliced onions until golden brown. Add ginger, garlic, green chilies, and curry leaves. Cook well.

STEP 3

Add turmeric, chili, coriander, and garam masala powders. Sauté until raw smell disappears.

STEP 4

Add chopped tomatoes and cook until mushy. Add thin coconut milk and let it simmer.

STEP 5

Add the eggs, simmer for 5 minutes. Pour thick coconut milk and switch off heat. Garnish and serve.

Ingredients

  • 4–5 eggs, boiled
  • 2 onions, sliced
  • 1 tomato, chopped
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 2 green chilies, slit
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp chili powder
  • 1 tbsp coriander powder
  • 1/2 tsp garam masala
  • 1 cup thin coconut milk
  • 1/2 cup thick coconut milk
  • Curry leaves
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tbsp coconut oil

Tools

  • Saucepan
  • Boiling pot for eggs
  • Spatula
  • Knife and chopping board
